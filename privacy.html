Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all games and apps developed and published by Serhat Kızılkaya (also known as Fourty Poles Studio), including: Pyramidka: Ring Stack Puzzle, 2000 IQ! - Tricky Puzzles, Nut Out! - Screw Puzzle, Stickman: Going Home, and DUEVO. Sections marked ★ DUEVO apply only to the DUEVO app.

1. Information We Collect — Games

Our games may collect the following data automatically through third-party services:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • Gameplay and app usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • General location (country/region level only)

We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or any personally identifiable information directly through our games.

2. Information We Collect — ★ DUEVO

DUEVO collects the following information to provide its accounting and finance features:

Account & Identity

  • Email address, display name, and Google account ID (via Google Sign-In)

User-Entered Data

  • Chart of accounts, income and expense records
  • Company name and business details
  • Invoice images and PDF files uploaded by the user

Automatically Collected

  • Device information and app version
  • Crash reports and app usage statistics (via Firebase)
  • Subscription and purchase status (via RevenueCat)

3. How We Use Your Information — ★ DUEVO

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • To store and sync your financial records across devices
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

5. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Some of our games and DUEVO offer optional in-app purchases or subscriptions. All transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store payment card information on our own servers.

For issues with purchases, contact us at fourtypolesstudio@gmail.com or reach out to Apple / Google support directly.

6. Biometric Authentication ★ DUEVO

DUEVO may offer optional biometric authentication (fingerprint / Face ID) as a security feature. Biometric verification happens entirely on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit your fingerprint or face data. The app only receives a "verified / not verified" result from your device's operating system and never has access to the biometric data itself.

7. Advertising

Some of our games display ads via Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

DUEVO does not display any advertisements.

8. Data Retention

Analytics and crash data collected through Firebase is retained per Google's standard retention policies. For DUEVO, your account data stored in Supabase is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Your Rights — Data Deletion ★ DUEVO

In accordance with GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data

To request account deletion or a data export, contact us at fourtypolesstudio@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all games and apps developed and published by Serhat Kızılkaya (also known as Fourty Poles Studio), including: Pyramidka: Ring Stack Puzzle, 2000 IQ! - Tricky Puzzles, Nut Out! - Screw Puzzle, Stickman: Going Home, and DUEVO.

Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of data automatically through third-party services:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • Gameplay and app usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • General location (country/region level)

We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or any personally identifiable information directly.

In-App Purchases

Some of our apps offer optional in-app purchases (IAP) such as removing ads or unlocking premium features. All purchases are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store any payment information on our own servers. Purchases are subject to the respective platform's terms of service.

If you have issues with a purchase, please contact us at fourtypolesstudio@gmail.com or reach out to Apple / Google support directly.

Advertising

Some of our games display ads provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using your Advertising ID. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention

Data collected through Firebase and AdMob is retained according to the respective policies of Google. We do not store any user data on our own servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

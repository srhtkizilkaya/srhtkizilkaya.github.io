Deleting Your DUEVO Account

Applies to: DUEVO app

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your DUEVO account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the DUEVO app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm the action in the dialog that appears

What Gets Deleted

Your account and all of your data will be permanently and immediately deleted and cannot be recovered. This includes:

  • Business name and account information
  • Current accounts (receivables)
  • All records, income and expense entries
  • Uploaded invoices and documents
  • App settings and preferences

No Access to the App?

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us from your account's registered email address. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
